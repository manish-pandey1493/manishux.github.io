I was fortunate to have the opportunity to join Fusemachines as a UI/UX designer intern. During my internship , I was able to explore various design principles and participate in design challenges presented to us. This experience provided me with valuable opportunities for professional growth and development in the field of UI/UX design.

Design Challenge I Completed

As part of a challenge, I was tasked with designing a mobile application that would enable employees to reserve their seats prior to arriving at the office. The resulting design prototype that I created is showcased below.

Selecting a particular floor

  • Enabling employees across teams to select their desired floor.
  • Displaying the real-time seat availability for a specific floor.
  • Displaying the current availability of seats for the first and second half of the day with specific seat counts.

Upcoming Bookings

  • Providing employees with updates on their upcoming bookings.
  • Providing comprehensive information on upcoming bookings, including the selected shift and floor details.
  • Inclusion of a calendar feature to facilitate quick and convenient selection of booking dates.

Recent Notices & Notifications

  • Displaying recent notices on the homepage to prevent employee oversight.
  • Older notifications and notices are viewable on the notifications screen.
