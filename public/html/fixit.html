Fixit

Passion Project • UX Design • Research • Targeted Region - Nepal

Facilitating the identification and selection of the most suitable technician for repairing household appliances through a mobile application.

Project Timeline

  • December 2021
  • 8 Weeks

My Role

  • Solo UX Designer
  • User Research

Tools Used

  • Figma
  • Google Forms
  • Figjam
  • Google Meet

Problem

Locating a suitable technician online in Nepal presents a significant challenge for many individuals.

One of the users, Lebron says - "I am a full-time college professor who wants to fix home appliances at weekends, but there isn't any platform to find reliable technicians to fix them."

Project Outcome

Showing technicians around you

  • Enabling users to either manually select a desired location or utilize their device's current location to display nearby technicians.
  • Locate me option allows users to pin their live location and list technicians that are within 5km of their location
  • Instant location switching within the app to find technicians for friends and families.

Quick Search

  • Searching technicians quickly and effectively.
  • Filter option to show only relevant technicians by searching pattern.
  • Incorporating categories for searching additional technicians and facilitating comparisons within the app could provide users with a more comprehensive experience.

In-app message requests

  • An automated message is implemented for faster request.
  • Categories to quickly sort previously sent messages for future recalling.
  • Options to send images of appliances for better problem clarification.

Design Approach

During the project, I tried using the double diamond approach. I wanted to explore a wide range of possibilities(divergent thinking), and then narrow down to the most promising solutions(convergent thinking), which is why I used this method.

Ideas I came up with

Some of the ideas with it's strengths and shortcomings are described below for better clarification.

Design Idea - 1 : Quick Search

Strength of the idea:

  • Easy for searching people and places
  • Finding right technician for the specific issue by just typing particular issue.
  • Use of the suggested keywords in search page for faster search.

Shortcomings:

  • Mismatching of the suggested keywords to that of user entered.
  • More options in the search page may create a dilemma in the user for figuring out their specific issue.

Solution:

Including filter option to sort out technicians based on user's specific needs.

Design Idea - 2 : Choosing Specific Location

Strength of the idea:

  • Helps to locate technicians that are around the user's location.
  • Services are not circumscribed to a particular area which can let more user base.
  • No compulsion to turn on user's location as they can choose from the cities listed in the app.

Shortcomings:

  • Need user's permission to allow precise location services.
  • More steps to be performed in order to locate user's location.

Solution:

After trying several design ideas, I decided to include both pre-defined location such as cities and an option for user to locate themselves.

Design Idea - 3 : Add to favorites from the quick profile view

Strength of the idea:

  • Users can easily add popular technicians to their favorites list for looking up their profile later.
  • More exposure to the technicians, as the tendency to recall technicians from the favorites list are always high.
  • Easy for users to recall and share the technician's profile in near future.

Reflections

This was one of my passion project which I had been working on for some time. It was a valuable learning experience for me as I had to independently manage every aspect of it. Here are some of the key takeaways I got through this project :

  • Design with confidence(Data-driven) :

    • In future projects, I would focus more on gathering user insights with statistical data, that will allow me to iteratively design with more confidence. Additionally, I plan to prioritize usability testing and extract insights from real users to ensure optimal user experience.

  • Iterate as early as you can during the process :

    • Based on my experience with this project, I have realized that there is a direct correlation between the time spent on iteration and the cost incurred. Therefore, it is more beneficial to iterate on designs during the early stages in order to save on production costs and time.
